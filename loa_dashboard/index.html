📄 LOA Request Process leads in success
LOA Request Process achieves 94% success rate across 305 cases — the highest of all use cases. It's predominantly used by Hourly Associates in Retail (272 of 305 cases) and completes in just 83 seconds on average.
📉 Policy Assistant drives GO & Office
169 of 365 Policy Assistant cases come from the GO line of business, with 133 from Office/division 0060. Its 43% success rate reflects complex, multi-turn interactions averaging 7.0 turns and 218 seconds.
📱 Multi-use-case sessions growing
189 of 700 sessions (27%) used multiple use cases in the same conversation. This indicates users increasingly chain Policy Assistant → LOA Request Process workflows as confidence in the platform grows.
